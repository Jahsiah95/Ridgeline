SERVICES

Design Calculations

Design Calculations are at the core of what we do as a business. They are the evidence used to determine whether a design will work or not. We provide calculations for any structural engineering project, large or small, across a wide range of domestic projects.

Structural Drawings

Building Information Modelling (BIM) is a modern process of providing computer files in typically proprietary formats containing data relating to the physical properties of a design. It is used as a tool in the design of projects, buildings and infrastructure in order to portray the vision and intent before ever commencing construction.
